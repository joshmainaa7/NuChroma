Why we're building this

The world has platforms for selling.
Creatives need one for thriving.

Existing platforms treat creatives as a sales channel, a casting list, or a gallery nobody visits. NuChroma is the operating system for the people who actually make things.

The landscape today

Good intentions. Missing pieces.

Dead portfolios

Behance · Dribbble

Static image grids built for 2012. Motion designers, sound artists, interactive creators — their work doesn't fit. Projects get 20 views after hours of layout work. Discovery is broken, and the algorithm rewards popularity, not craft.

Pay to be seen

Backstage

$20/month to access mostly unpaid roles. Rampant spam from fake casting directors. Hostile billing practices. A platform built around auditions, not around artists — and limited almost entirely to the US and UK.

You're the product, not the customer

Twiva · Influencer platforms

Creators are a distribution channel for brands. No portfolio. No creative identity. Campaign-only earnings that disappear between gigs. 60% of African creators earn under $100/month — the infrastructure, not the talent, is what's missing.

No business layer

All of them

The moment money enters the conversation, you leave. Briefs happen in email. Contracts happen in Google Docs. Payments happen in PayPal. Nobody owns the creative pipeline from "nice work" to "here's your payment."

Built for San Francisco

All of them

Payment rails assume Stripe in USD. Tax templates assume W-9s. The creative scenes in Nairobi, Lagos, Accra, and Johannesburg are exploding — but existing platforms weren't built with them in mind.

Collaboration is invisible

All of them

Creative work is a team sport — director, DP, editor, composer. But no platform lets you credit collaborators, pitch as a team, or show the network behind the work. Your best projects look solo even when they weren't.

The NuChroma way

What we're building instead.

01

Portfolios that move

Drag-and-drop case studies with video, audio, 3D embeds, and narrative flow. Your profile is a living showcase — not a grid of thumbnails cropped to fit someone else's template.

02

Business built into the conversation

Briefs, rate cards, milestone contracts, and payments — all inside DMs. From "love your work" to "invoice paid" without ever leaving NuChroma. A fair, sliding fee that shrinks as you earn.

03

Signal, not noise

Likes are private. We surface what matters: saves, outreach, repeat views, projects landed. Your reputation is built on real hiring signal, not vanity counts.

04

Discovery that actually works

Search by discipline, tools, location, language, rate range, and availability. Find "a Swahili-speaking motion designer in Nairobi available next month" in seconds, not hours.

05

Team credits, not solo myths

Tag every collaborator. Show the team behind the work. Pitch as a crew. Creative work is collective — NuChroma makes that visible instead of hiding it.

06

Built from Nairobi, not Silicon Valley

M-Pesa, mobile money, multi-currency support from day one. Local contract templates. Local payment rails. Built for the creative markets the rest of the world is sleeping on.
